What is CodeChallenge.io

Our goal at CodeChallenge.io is to help people who are interested in coding/programming, to improve their skills. We will provide challenges and projects on different complexities, and on a lot of different subjects including web design/development (jQuery, JS, CSS, HTML etc.). Our format is very simple, there will be text writing about the project, a video explainig and some links/resources to help you build it. Finally you will submit your project through a link (Github, Codepen etc.) and proceed to the next progressively harder challenge.
